Agrume Design System

Mint-lagoon identity with soft gold, blush accents, and structured pastels. Dark-first, responsive, and accessible.

115+

Character DNA

Agrume translates OC identity into reusable UI rules: mint hair energy, lagoon fabric depth, soft gold accessories, and blush emotion as a secondary accent.

Color Story

Primary interactions stay mint-lagoon. Gold marks key highlights. Blush appears in emotional details and never dominates layout.

Mint Lagoon Lagoon Blue Soft Gold Blush Accent

Shape Language

Rounded cards, gentle depth, and soft motifs keep interfaces cute but structured. Decorative patterns remain in presentation areas only.

Motion Tone

Motion is subtle and alive: short transitions, clear feedback, and reduced-motion respect for accessibility by default.

Do

  • Use mint-lagoon for primary actions and active states.
  • Keep blush for tags, highlights, and emotional micro-details.
  • Prefer soft elevation over strong neon glow.

Do Not

  • Turn blush into a dominant background color.
  • Stack heavy animations on every component.
  • Use decorative motifs in dense data tables or forms.
